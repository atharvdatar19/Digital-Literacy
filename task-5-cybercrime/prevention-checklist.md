# Stay Safe Online Checklist

- [ ] **Use Strong, Unique Passwords:** Use a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols. Consider a password manager.
- [ ] **Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):** Turn it on for email, social media, and banking accounts.
- [ ] **Keep Software Updated:** Regularly update your OS, antivirus, and applications to patch vulnerabilities.
- [ ] **Beware of Phishing Emails:** Verify the sender's address and do not click on suspicious links or download unknown attachments.
- [ ] **Use Secure Connections:** Avoid public Wi-Fi for sensitive transactions; use a trusted VPN if necessary.
- [ ] **Review Privacy Settings:** Regularly check and adjust social media privacy settings.
- [ ] **Backup Your Data:** Keep copies of important files on an external drive or secure cloud storage.
- [ ] **Verify E-commerce Sites:** Ensure the website uses HTTPS and is legitimate before entering payment details.
