## 📌 Project Overview

This Digital Literacy Portfolio is developed as part of the CSE0001 Digital Literacy course at VIT Bhopal University to demonstrate essential skills required in today's digital environment. The project focuses on building awareness about responsible digital usage, professional communication, online presence, and cyber safety.

Through a series of structured tasks, this portfolio highlights practical knowledge of digital tools, collaboration platforms, coding practice, and ethical online behaviour. It reflects the role of a **Student Digital Ambassador** in guiding peers toward effective and safe use of digital technologies.
