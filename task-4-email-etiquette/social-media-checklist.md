# Social Media Do's and Don'ts

## Do's
- **Be Respectful:** Always communicate politely and professionally.
- **Fact-Check:** Verify information before sharing or retweeting.
- **Protect Privacy:** Keep personal information (address, phone number) private.
- **Use Strong Passwords:** Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA).
- **Think Before You Post:** Remember that the internet is forever.

## Don'ts
- **Don't Cyberbully:** Avoid participating in harassment or toxic arguments.
- **Don't Overshare:** Avoid posting sensitive personal details.
- **Don't Click Suspicious Links:** Be wary of phishing attempts in direct messages.
- **Don't Plagiarize:** Always give credit when sharing others' work.
- **Don't Engage with Trolls:** Ignore and block abusive accounts.
